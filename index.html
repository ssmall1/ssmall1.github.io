More about me

I'm currently in Chicago, Illinois

After graduating from UCLA in June, 2020. I decided my future impact on the world would be best served by combining my knowledge and experience of environmental business and consulting with the impact-driven potential of software development. I'm excited about all the positive change we can manifest if we work together!

I mostly work on projects that highlight what I love about the world, as well as anything that improves humans' quality of life or takes care of the planet giving us life. When I'm not programming, I'm usually playing soccer, cultivating my garden, chasing delicious food, or absorbing stories from every medium.
